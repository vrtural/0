This is a digital letter creation and sharing platform operated by TheanhVo.

Features

  • Letters expire after 7 days.
  • Max 3 active letters system-wide.
  • 6-char key with 2B+ combos for security.

How It Works

This is a service where traditional letters are brought back to life.

  1. 1. You start by clicking the Request Service button, which opens an email draft to us with a preset format.
  2. 2. After sending, you’ll receive an email confirming that your request is under review. We check available slots and fix any issues.
  3. 3. If your request is approved, you’ll get a success email with a customer code. If not, you’ll get a failure notification.
  4. 4. The success email will ask you for the letter’s title and content. You can write it yourself or ask AI for help.
  5. 5. After you reply with the needed info, we prepare your letter and send you a final email with a link to your completed letter.
